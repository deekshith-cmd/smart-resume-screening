## 🆘 Need Help?

**Common Issues:**

A: Follow steps in `RENDER_DEPLOY.md` Section 2

**Q: Deployment failed**
A: Check Render logs, ensure all files uploaded

**Q: App is slow**

**Q: Want app always active?**
A: Use PythonAnywhere or upgrade Render to paid
